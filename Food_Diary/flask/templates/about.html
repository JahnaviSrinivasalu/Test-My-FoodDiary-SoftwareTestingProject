Food diary is a super easy food/diet journal.
Food diary is a daily log of what you eat and drink each day.It allows users to track their food intake. The diary helps you understand your eating habits. It can help you realize what you consume.Once you know this,you can make changes to your diet to improve your health.Studies have found that people who have healthy diet goals are more successful when they keep a FOOD DIARY.
